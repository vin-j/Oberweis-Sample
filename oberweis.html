About


What: Oberweis is a family-owned dairy company famous for its premium ice cream, milk, and other dairy products. Founded: 1927 in Aurora, Illinois. Products: Ice cream, milk, chocolate milk, butter, cheese, yogurt, and more—all made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Business Model: They operate retail stores, including ice cream and dairy shops, and also offer home delivery in some areas. Reputation: Known for rich, creamy ice cream and fresh, wholesome dairy, emphasizing no artificial ingredients or preservatives. Expansion: Oberweis has grown beyond Illinois and nearby states, but it’s still mostly regional in the Midwest and parts of the U.S.
